About Harrisonburg

Located between the Blue Ridge and Allegheny Mountain ranges in the western part of Virginia, Harrisonburg is a hidden gem. With Shenandoah Valley at its doorstep, you do not have to look far to find breathtaking views and activities to do in nature. Visitors are welcomed by a diverse and vibrant downtown as well.
